About Water Intake

The human body is composed of about 60% water, and maintaining proper hydration is essential for numerous bodily functions including temperature regulation, joint lubrication, and nutrient transportation.

Hydration Tips

1
Carry a water bottle - Having water readily available makes it easier to stay hydrated throughout the day.
2
Monitor urine color - Pale yellow indicates proper hydration, while dark yellow suggests you need more water.
3
Eat water-rich foods - Fruits and vegetables like cucumbers, watermelon, and oranges contribute to hydration.
4
Set reminders - Use apps or alarms to remind yourself to drink water regularly.

Note: Individual water needs can vary based on factors like health conditions, pregnancy, or breastfeeding. Consult with a healthcare provider for personalized recommendations.
