Why Protein Matters

Protein is essential for building and repairing tissues, producing enzymes and hormones, and supporting overall muscle health. It's especially important for active individuals and those pursuing fitness goals.

Protein Tips

1
Distribute intake evenly - Aim to consume protein throughout the day to maximize absorption.
2
Include complete sources - Choose high-quality proteins like eggs, poultry, fish, and legumes.
3
Track your intake - Use apps or logs to ensure you're meeting your daily protein needs.

Note: Individual protein needs can vary based on factors like health conditions, age, or specific dietary requirements. Consult with a healthcare provider or nutritionist for personalized recommendations.
